I am a second year PhD student in mathematics at the University of California, Berkeley. My advisor is
[Prof. Sug Woo Shin](https://math.berkeley.edu/~swshin/). My research interests are in number theory. Recently, I have been thinking about local-global compatibility questions arising in the Langlands program. 

Prior to this, I completed my undergraduate studies at the Indian Institute of Science and completed the Part III at the University of Cambridge.
